# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the BOSC Community Library a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at bosc-maintainers@gmail.com.
